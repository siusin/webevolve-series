Event summary

Overview

WebEvolve-2024 Annual Conference took place during 28-29 May. The event revolved around the themes of Machine Learning, WebGPU and Media technologies, with a focus on discussing multimedia technologies, media transport, machine learning, metaverse, immersive Web, WebGPU and related standardization activities on Web.

The event was hosted by W3C China (Beihang University). Many thanks to bilibili, Khronos Group and Microsoft Reactor for co-organizing this event.

The event was held in hybrid mode, with the main in-person hub in Shanghai. Over 90 participants attended the event on site, with approximately 10,000 watches through the live webcast platforms including bilibili, WeChat channels, CSDN, and SegmentFault. The audience participated and interacted activele during the live webcasts. We extend our gratitude to all the speakers for their excellent presentations and to all attendees for their support and participation.

Talks summary

Topics discussed during the event are summarized as follows, including public-vislbe slides and recordings authorized by speakers.

Media and Entertainment on the Web: François Daoust (W3C Media Specialist) 分享了 W3C 在实时媒体、Web 游戏、AI、元宇宙等领域的标准化探索与当前工作进展 (See slides, video will be uploaded).

WebCodecs opens a new chapter in Web audio and video: Jun Liu (senior engineer at bilibili) 重点介绍了 WebCodecs，分享了 WebCodecs 能做什么、它的应用场景及其优势与限制 (See slides, video to be uploaded).

Vulkan Synchronization for WebGPU: Nathan Li (Senior Manager of Developer Ecology at ARM) 对比了 WebGPU 与 WebGL、Vulkan 之间的关系，介绍了 Dawn 项目（一个围绕 GPU 的实验），并分享了相关的开发者资源 (See slides, video will be uploaded).

Update on WebGPU and Web AI: Yang Gu (responsible for Web Graphics and AI at Intel) 分享了 WebGPU API 和 WebGPU 着色语言规范的核心进展，当前主流浏览器的支持状态，以及开发中的新特性（尤其是 AI 相关的特性）(See slides, video will be uploaded).

WebRTC media transport explorations and signaling standardization applications: Cheng Chen (senior engineer of WebRTC transmission at ByteDance) 分享了对 WebRTC 媒体传输的探索（包括 WebRTC 媒体传输流程、WebRTC Insertable Streams、Unbundling WebRTC），以及 WebRTC 信令标准化应用（包括 WHIP/WHEP 协议和应用场景）(See slides, video will be uploaded).

Overview and update on WebNN: Ningxin Hu (Principal Engineer at Intel Web Platform Engineering team) 展示了 CPU、GPU、NPU 这三个 AI PC 的硬件引擎，阐释了 WebNN 为 Web 带来了神经网络的统一抽象，通过原生 OS ML API 访问 AI 硬件加速，提供接近原生的性能和下一代用例，并分享了相关规范的开发进度及实现状态 (See slides, video on bilibili).

ncnn Vulkan Machine Learning Update：Hui Ni (researcher at Tencent Youtu Lab) 概述了 ncnn 神经网络推理框架，介绍了基于 Vulkan 的机器学习相关进展 (See slides, video on bilibili).

WebXR practices in 3D engine: Qianwei Xu (Galacean 3D Interactive Engine team lead at Ant Group) 展示了 XR 相关的业务诉求，分享了 Web 3D 引擎（Galacean Engine）、为什么选择 WebXR 以及 Web 3D 引擎中的 XR 框架设计，并介绍了正在进行的客户端 WebXR 基建与 XR 编辑器等工作 (See slides, video on bilibili).

Practices of Web Media Processing and Real-time Communication Standards: Chun Gao (senior architect at Shengwang) 分享了 RTC 行业的新趋势，重点分析了端到端加密、数字版权管理、WebRTC H265 支持、Alpha 视频传输等案例 (See slides, video to be uploaded).

Matroska unpacking principles and practices: Yanjun Wang (senior engineer at bilibili) 分享了 Matroska 解封装的背景、原理、方案、应用，并展望了如何进一步优化解析流程和提升解析能力 (See slides, video to be uploaded).

Exploration and practices of Secure Camera: Wu Zhang (senior technical expert at IIFAA Alliance) 探索了在应对 DeepFake 实时图像流注入攻击风险时，如何通过安全摄像头的方式提升整个图片流的可信度和安全性 (See slides, video on bilibili).

Exploration on next generation of Internet - Interplanetary Network: Song Xu（Technical Director of China Mobile Migu) 分享了人类对太空互联网的探索活动、星际通讯面临的挑战，以及探索建设下一代太空互联网需要考虑的问题。

Exploration and practice of Immersive Web API: Yifei Yu (technical expert at ByteDance) 分享了享字节跳动跨平台团队对沉浸式 WebAPI 的探索、实现和实际应用 (See slides, video on bilibili).

Building a brand new ecosystem with OpenXR: Shuai Liu (XRRuntime tech lead for PICO at ByteDance) 重点介绍了 OpenXR 的基础概念与应用、OpenXR API 的概况、OpenXR 1.1 规范的近期更新以及未来的进一步扩展 (See slides, video on bilibili).

Vulkan standard and its progress: Kangying Cai (senior researcher of graphics standards at Huawei) 分享了图形 API 的演进，将传统与现代图形 API 之间进行了对比，重点介绍了 Vulkan API 及其2024年的发展路线图。

HDR Vivid and Audio Vivid technical standards and related applications: Yun Zhou (senior engineer at the Academy of Broadcasting Science, National Radio and Television Administration, NRTA) 分享了 HDR Vivid 和 Audio Vivid 技术架构、关键技术、方案特点、应用场景以及标准所面临的问题，并围绕 HDR 和空间音频体验在 Web 上的应用提出了思考。

Embeddable WebGL + WebXR binocular implementation: Yazhong Liu（technical expert at Rokid) 介绍了空间小程序、统一渲染、WebXR 统一渲染的实现路径以及未来计划（空间中的浏览器）。

AI enables intelligent production of accessibility films: Wei Wang (senior engineer at Zhejiang University) 阐释了无障碍电影、无障碍直播解说及其制作流程，探讨了优化电影字幕识别能力的方向，以及人工智能如何进一步赋能无障碍电影制作流程，并展示了目前已经取得的成果 (See slides, video on bilibili).

Web-based 3D digital human development and practice: Lei Zhao (Frontend Development Director at China Mobile Migu) 分享了数智人的概念、技术开发与相关实践，展望了 WebGPU 与 3D 渲染、生成式 AI 和渲染结合的未来前景 (See slides, video on bilibili).

Application practices of front-end in digital human authoring tools: Bean Deng (senior engineer at bilibili) 展示了基于音色克隆和数字分身技术的“必剪Studio”智能创作平台，介绍了绿幕抠像的原理、流程、技术挑战和应对方案，以及音视频合成、音频波形可视化、音频在线转码、SSML 可视化编辑器等应用实践 (See slides, video on bilibili).
